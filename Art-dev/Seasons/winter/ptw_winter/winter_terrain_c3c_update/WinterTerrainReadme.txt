C3C Update for Winter Terrain mod by embryodead

Put these files into "Winter Terrain/Art/Terrain" folder.
This is what you will get:
- snowy marshes (a good name for them would be "Ice Bogs")
- snowy volcanos
- new tnt.pcx (grasslands with shields will show small trees instead of normally invisible white shield)
- sea landmark - iceberg (LM Sea is tricky - you have place few squares to make the iceberg show up)
- white craters (brown look strange on winter terrain)

I also included Warpstorm's fix for those pink lines that appear with LM terrain in the editor.
